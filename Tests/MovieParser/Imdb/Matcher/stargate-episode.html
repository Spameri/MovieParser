Storyline

After the battle, Earth's forces and those of their allies regroup. Teal'c is a prisoner of the Lucian Alliance who he convinced the join the attack but now think they were fooled into joining them so as to have their fleet destroyed. Daniel thinks he knows what Merlin's weapon looks like but no one seems to know where it is to be found. On board one of the Ori vessels, Vala gives birth to a baby girl - who very quickly grows into a preteen. She turns out to be an Ori in human form and Vala hopes to convince her that the Ori are evil. It's clearly not going to easy. Written by garykmcd

Goofs

When Colonel Mitchell "request permission to come on board" and permission is granted, the officer next to the Captain states "Left docking bay is out of commission Colonel".

A crew member of a ship (marine, air or space) would always refer to sides of a craft as either Port or Starboard. Left and Right are terms that can be easily confused as they are relative to the direction a person is facing, which may be opposite to the direction of the ship. Whereas Port and Starboard are fixed to the ship regardless of which direction the ship is facing. See more »
